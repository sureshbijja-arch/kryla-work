# Kryla.work — Go-To-Market Playbook

## The Most Important Rule

**The first 10 members cannot be acquired by platform marketing.**

They require a human who believes in the product. Walk in. Sit with them. Build the profile in front of them. This is non-negotiable.

Why: The first 10 members are proof. Without proof, no channel works. With proof, every channel works.

## 90-Day Sequence

### Week 1–2: Hand-to-hand
**Celina, TX:**
- Walk into 20 local businesses (tutors, bakers, trainers, salons, photographers)
- Offer to build their profile for free, in front of them, in 15 minutes
- Target: 10 live profiles, at least 3 who get their first booking

**India:**
- Join 10 WhatsApp groups (tutor groups, home baker groups, colony resident groups)
- Observe only. Do not pitch. Understand the language they use.

### Week 3–4: Social proof
**Celina:**
- Post on Nextdoor + Celina Facebook groups
- Share the actual member profiles, not ads ("Meet Priya, a maths tutor in Celina — she's now booking students online")
- Let the work speak. No marketing copy.

**India:**
- Share first 3 profiles in WhatsApp groups — "A friend built this"
- Watch what people say about it

### Month 2: Referral
- Launch: "Bring a friend to Kryla, get 1 month free"
- Every existing member gets a personal referral link
- WhatsApp them personally to announce — not a mass email

**India:**
- Identify 3 "colony ambassadors" — one connected person in each target city
- Give them free Sprout for 6 months in exchange for 10 referrals

### Month 3: Institutional
**Celina:**
- Celina ISD parent newsletter (tutors angle)
- Celina Chamber of Commerce (small business angle)
- Collin County SBDC partnership

**India:**
- First MSME WhatsApp group partnership
- Hindi YouTube: "15 minute mein apna online presence banao" (15 min to build your online presence)

### Month 4–6: Double down
- Identify top 2 channels from data
- Put 80% of effort into those 2

**India:**
- Physical visit to Tier 2 city (Pune, Jaipur, or Coimbatore)
- Partner with 1 local co-working space

### Month 7–12: Flywheel
- SEO starts compounding (member profiles indexed by Google)
- Referral program self-sustaining
- YouTube/content SEO (Hindi + English)

## Channels That Work for Kryla

| Channel | Why it works | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Walking in | Trust. They see it built. | Time only |
| Nextdoor | Hyper-local. Celina residents trust it. | Free |
| Facebook local groups | Where small business owners live | Free |
| WhatsApp groups | India primary channel | Free |
| Referrals | Member-to-member trust | 1 month free |
| ISD/Chamber | Institutional credibility | Time only |
| Hindi YouTube | India: massive underserved audience | Production time |
| Google (SEO) | Compounding, member profiles rank | Time (Month 4+) |

## Channels That DON'T Work (yet)

- Google Ads — too expensive before LTV is proven
- Instagram ads — wrong audience for our personas
- Cold email — tutors/bakers don't respond
- Product Hunt — not the audience we need first

## Conversion Funnel

```
Hears about Kryla
    ↓ (40% drop — never visit)
Visits kryla.work
    ↓ (60% drop — don't start onboarding)
Starts onboarding
    ↓ (30% drop — don't complete)
Completes onboarding → profile live
    ↓ (KEY MOMENT: first booking)
Stays active → converts to paid
```

**The fix for each drop:**
- Drop 1 (never visit): Show them a real peer profile, not an ad. "Here's Meena's profile."
- Drop 2 (don't start): Make the CTA specific. Not "Get started" — "See your name on kryla.work/[yourname]"
- Drop 3 (don't complete): 5 questions max. Show progress. Auto-save.
- Drop 4 (no first booking): Member gets WhatsApp coaching — "Share your link with 3 people today"

## Scripts

### Walking into a Celina business
"Hi, I'm building a tool that helps [tutors/bakers/trainers] get found online in Celina. I'd love to build you a free profile — takes 15 minutes — and you'll have your own link you can share with customers. Can I show you?"

### WhatsApp group intro (India)
"Hi everyone — I'm working on something for [tutors/home bakers] who want to be found online without needing a website. Would anyone be open to trying it for free? Takes 15 minutes."

### Referral ask to existing member
"Priya, you've had 7 bookings this month through Kryla. If you know another tutor who could use this, send them your link — you both get a free month."
