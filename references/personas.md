# Kryla.work — Target Personas

## Scoring Framework

Each persona scored on:
- **Pain** (1–10): How badly do they need an online presence?
- **Readiness** (1–10): How tech-comfortable? Will they complete onboarding?
- **ARPU** (1–10): Willingness and ability to pay?
- **Market size**: How many of them exist?

## Phase 1 — Validate (build for these first)

### 1. Home Tuition Teacher
- **Pain:** 9/10 — No website. Students find them through WhatsApp chains and word of mouth only.
- **Readiness:** 8/10 — Comfortable with smartphones. Uses Google Forms, WhatsApp.
- **ARPU:** 7/10 — India: ₹299-799/mo easy. USA: $5-12/mo easy.
- **Market:** 38M+ in India. 500K+ in USA (home tutoring market).
- **Why start here:** 92% have no website. Both India and Celina markets. Founder's kid uses a tutor — living proof of the persona.
- **Key pain:** "I have to explain my qualifications every time someone new WhatsApps me."

### 2. Independent Fitness Trainer
- **Pain:** 8/10 — Post-COVID boom. Training clients from home or at client's home. No studio, no web presence.
- **Readiness:** 9/10 — Very smartphone-comfortable. Uses Instagram already.
- **ARPU:** 8/10 — Higher income, willing to invest in professional presence.
- **Market:** 800K+ certified trainers in USA. 2M+ in India.
- **Key pain:** "I lose clients to trainers with better Instagram pages, even though I'm better."

### 3. Home Baker
- **Pain:** 9/10 — Instagram DM chaos. Orders coming in across 3 platforms. No single place to send people.
- **Readiness:** 8/10 — Social media savvy. Will complete onboarding.
- **ARPU:** 6/10 — Lower margins on baked goods, but high volume.
- **Market:** 1M+ home bakers in USA. 5M+ in India.
- **Key pain:** "I'm managing orders on WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, and phone calls. I miss orders."

### 4. Freelance Photographer
- **Pain:** 9/10 — Instagram is not a portfolio. No booking system. Pricing is hidden.
- **Readiness:** 9/10 — Highly tech-comfortable. Already invests in their brand.
- **ARPU:** 9/10 — Higher price point. Wedding photographers especially.
- **Market:** 250K+ freelance photographers in USA. 500K+ in India.
- **Key pain:** "I send clients to my Instagram and they can't find my pricing or how to book me."

## Phase 2 — Scale

### 5. Independent Salon / Beauty Professional
- **Pain:** 8/10 — Appointment chaos. Walk-ins vs bookings.
- **Readiness:** 7/10 — Comfortable with booking apps, will use Kryla.
- **ARPU:** 8/10 — Good income, willing to pay for bookings.
- **Celina fit:** High — Celina has 50+ independent salons and beauty professionals.

### 6. Music Teacher
- **Pain:** 8/10 — Very similar to tutor. Batch management chaos.
- **Readiness:** 7/10 — Older demographic, slightly less tech-ready.
- **ARPU:** 7/10 — Steady income, willing to pay.

### 7. Wedding Videographer
- **Pain:** 9/10 — High-value bookings lost due to poor web presence.
- **Readiness:** 9/10 — Very tech-comfortable.
- **ARPU:** 10/10 — Highest price point. One booking pays for a year of Thrive.

### 8. Home Chef / Tiffin Service
- **Pain:** 9/10 — Managing orders entirely on WhatsApp. No menu link to share.
- **Readiness:** 6/10 — Less tech-comfortable. Needs simpler onboarding.
- **ARPU:** 5/10 — Lower margins. Price-sensitive.
- **India fit:** Extremely high. Tiffin services are massive in Tier 2 cities.

## Phase 3 — Expand (higher ARPU, more complex)

### Doctor / Physiotherapist
- **Pain:** 7/10 — Appointment management. Trust-building online.
- **Readiness:** 8/10 — Professional, tech-comfortable.
- **ARPU:** 10/10 — Highest willingness to pay.
- **Challenge:** Regulatory. Medical claims need careful handling.

### CA / Accountant
- **Pain:** 7/10 — No web presence. Clients find them through referrals only.
- **ARPU:** 9/10 — High income, professional.
- **India fit:** Very high. 800K+ CAs in India.

### Interior Designer / Decorator
- **Pain:** 8/10 — Portfolio-first business. Instagram not enough.
- **ARPU:** 8/10 — Project-based, high value.

### Dietitian / Nutritionist
- **Pain:** 8/10 — Post-COVID health boom. No web presence.
- **ARPU:** 7/10 — Good income. Willing to invest.

## Phase 4 — Volume Play (lower ARPU, higher volume)

### Restaurant / Cafe
- **Pain:** 6/10 — Google Maps partially solves this.
- **ARPU:** 4/10 — Price-sensitive. Compete on margin.
- **Volume:** Massive market but low per-unit economics.

### Repair Shop / Electrician / Plumber
- **Pain:** 7/10 — Locally invisible. Found only by word of mouth.
- **ARPU:** 4/10 — Very price-sensitive.
- **Volume:** Enormous market in both India and USA.

## Celina, TX Specific Personas (22 of 35 apply)

Celina is ideal for: tutors, trainers, photographers, salons, home bakers, music teachers, wedding vendors, interior designers, home chefs, dietitians, yoga teachers, dance teachers, language teachers, speech therapists, childcare providers, lawn care, house cleaners, handymen, electricians, plumbers, auto detailers, dog trainers.

**Why Celina:** 3,000 new homes/year. Young families. Dual income. Education-focused. The founder lives here — ground-level access.
