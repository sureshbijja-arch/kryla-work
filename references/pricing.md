# Kryla.work — Pricing & Unit Economics

## Plan Summary

| Plan | India/mo | USA/mo | Key unlock |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🌱 Seed | Free | Free | Profile + contact form |
| 🌿 Sprout | ₹299 | $5 | Booking + WhatsApp alerts |
| 🌳 Grow | ₹799 | $12 | Custom domain + analytics |
| 🚀 Thrive | ₹1,999 | $25 | All features + WhatsApp updates |
| ⭐ Elevate | ₹3,999 | $45 | Payments + team + branded email |

## What Each Plan Includes

### 🌱 Seed — Free forever
- AI-generated profile (one build)
- Contact form (10 inquiries/month cap)
- Directory listing
- Email notifications only (no WhatsApp)
- kryla.work/[slug] URL

### 🌿 Sprout — ₹299 / $5 per month
Everything in Seed, plus:
- Booking form (unlimited)
- WhatsApp alerts when new business comes in
- Customer confirmation messages
- Remove inquiry cap

### 🌳 Grow — ₹799 / $12 per month — MOST POPULAR
Everything in Sprout, plus:
- Custom domain (priya.com → your profile)
- Analytics (who looked you up, where from)
- Monthly SEO refresh
- Uptime monitoring
- Choose your own profile photo
- Gallery (up to 10 photos)

### 🚀 Thrive — ₹1,999 / $25 per month
Everything in Grow, plus:
- Update your profile via WhatsApp ("change my price to ₹600")
- Review collection from customers
- All 6 AI agents running
- Priority support

### ⭐ Elevate — ₹3,999 / $45 per month
Everything in Thrive, plus:
- Online payments (collect deposits or full payment)
- Team access (up to 3 people manage the profile)
- Branded email (priya@priya.com)
- Brand kit + logo
- Dedicated account manager

## Unit Economics (USA, fully automated)

| Plan | MRR | Infra | WhatsApp | AI agents | Margin |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Seed | $0 | $0.25 | $0 | $0.15 | -$0.40 (CAC) |
| Sprout | $5 | $0.30 | $0.50 | $0.55 | ~74% |
| Grow | $12 | $0.40 | $0.50 | $0.65 | ~76% |
| Thrive | $25 | $0.50 | $1.00 | $1.03 | ~83% |
| Elevate | $45 | $0.75 | $1.50 | $1.03 | ~88% |

**Seed members cost $0.40/mo.** This is the cost of customer acquisition — far cheaper than ads.
**Expected free-to-paid conversion: 15–25%.**

## Upgrade Hooks (how members naturally move up)

- **Seed → Sprout:** Hits 10 inquiry limit. Wants proper booking. "You've had 10 enquiries this month — ready to take proper bookings?"
- **Sprout → Grow:** Wants their own domain. "Ready for priya.com instead of priya.kryla.work?"
- **Grow → Thrive:** Wants to update profile via WhatsApp. Wants to collect reviews.
- **Thrive → Elevate:** Wants to take payments online. Has a team.

## Key Pricing Rules

- WhatsApp notifications: Sprout and above ONLY. Seed = email only.
- Custom domain: Grow and above ONLY.
- Online payments: Elevate ONLY.
- All 6 AI agents: Thrive and above.
- Never call Seed "Free plan" or "Trial" — it's Seed.
- Never say "Upgrade" — say "Grow your membership" or "Move up to [plan]".
